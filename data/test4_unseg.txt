Tunah chuan, chutiang chu ni tawh lovin, khawi emaw lai laiahte chuan tawng dang dang hmang lo la awm ve zauh zauh mah sela, chhim lama Lai, Mara leh Chakma te, Mizoram khawthlang hrûla Bru/Riang -hoten an awm khâwmnaa an tawng an hmang tih mai lohvah phei chuan, Mizo tawng hi mi tinin kan hman ber a lo ni ta a ni.</p>
<p>Mama, engti dan nge? I pa rui sual leh buaina siam i tawng em ni? A va pawi tak em aw.</p>
<p>Tawng hi ri a ni a. Ri hrang hrang inphuah khâwm khân awmzia a lo nei ta a. Tichuan, mihring leh mihring inkârah, inpawh tawnna a lo awm thei ta a ni. Tin, hêng rite hi, a tihrîkna hmun leh a tihrîk dana zirin hming vuah vek a ni. Mizo tawng consonant rite a awmze tidanglam theitute chu hengte hi a ni. Kan sawi tawh angin a chunga mite khi, Mizo tawnga consonant ri awmzia tidanglam theitu a ni. Tin, ph, th, kh, th, chh-a, h ri belhte hi aspiration a ni; chu chu, a lamrîk zâwnga boruak awm dan entir nân hman a ni a. Dân narânin, ri tihchhuah laia aspiration awm telna ri rêng rêng hian awmzia a tidanglam ngai lo. Mahse, Mizo tawngah chuan ri awmze tidanglam thei a ni. Tin, tawnga rite hi, Voiced leh voiceless ritea then an ni leh a. Rite hi ká aṭanga kan tihchhuah hian vocal cords awm dana zirin vibration a awmin a awm lo thei a ni. Vibration a awm chuan, kha ri kha voiced tia koh a ni a, Vibration a awm loh chuan, ri chu voiceless tih a ni. Hnár lam aṭanga ri tihchhuah m, n, ng, te hi voiced rite a ni a. Mizo tawngah chuan, a voiceless pawh a awm. Hemi danglamna tilang tur hian, kan Zosapte khân, remchang takin a voiceless entir nan h an lo hmang niin a lang.</p>
<p>He dan hian thil chi hrang hrang a huam thei a. Zirlai awlai aṭanga a harsa leh huphurhawm zawka kal te, thil pakhat aṭanga thil dang a kaihhnawih theiha luh te, hriat tawh sa aṭanga la hriat ngai loh zir thar te, a tlangpui sawi aṭanga pawimawh zual lai sawi chipchiar leh te a huam vek awm e.</p>
<p>Ka nu, Chhungtea chu a inhlawhna te'n chaw ei turin an ti nghal a ni ang, fel an ti riau asin.</p>
