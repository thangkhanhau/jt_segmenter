<p>Hei bak hi engmah dang Krismas lam sawi a nei lo. He Hrawva hla lehlinah hian, Ram loh tui thiang cham del chungah Berampa tuannaah tih kan hmu a; hla lehlinnaah pawh hla thu hmang thiam hmasa berte zinga mi a ni nghal bawk.</p>
<p>A pawilo, a pawi lo," tih tuma ka kâ ka ân mek laiin Dawngi chu choka kawngkaah chuan a rawn dâk a, nui deuh hian, "Ho mai mai. Ti khan lo thu rawh se. Nula rim tur pawh ni hlei lo," a ti a. <p>Thingzai kha chuan bûk fo teh suh u.</p>
<p>Mizo tawnga kan common noun thu malte hi a then chu thu lam khat chauh te a ni a, a then chu thu lam hnih leh a âia tam inkawp te a ni a. Common noun thu mal rau rau pawh a thu lam bi zât a inang hlawm lo a; chúng common noun chu a thu lam bi zât inang lo mah sela, thu mal pakhat-common noun-an nih dan erawh a inang vek thung a ni. Chúng common noun chu an kawppui noun thu mal dang nena ziah dan turah MLC-a kan pûte hi chuan grammar ni lo, teh fung dang bawk an rawn duang leh a. An zînga pakhatin MLC-ina Mizo tawng ziah dan tur a duannaah hian 'a lam rik zâtin ziah dan a hril thu' leh, chu chu MLC chuan teh funga an hman thu a sawi-kan tàr lan tâk-kha an lehkhabuah hian teh fung pawimawh takah an lo hmang tak tak hi a lo ni a!</p>
<p>Hei hi chawn hmahruai a ni a, ṭhenkhat chuan Dawino chhui hmain an hmang a. Vawkpa pahnih leh vawkpui pakhat an talh a. Chawng tûr pa kawtah zan tin nula leh tlangválin zû tur buh an dêng a. Zû chu ngán sawmli vêl an siam a, chúng buh dengtute tân chuan 'sumdêng zû' an tih, ngán sarih zêlin vawi hnih an thehsak ang. Chawnna tûra a zu bilh a thlum tikah chuan 'in chhe siam ni' an ti a, tlâng chim ngam tûrin upa-in an thawm tha a, chumi ni bawk chuan nula leh tlangvâlin chawn thing an phur a. An zu sak pawh chhawlhal dam chauhin an kai chhuak a. Mahse tlàwmngaiin an in duh lo va. Val-upain an inluihtir hrâm hrâm thina ni. Chawn huna awm dan tur mawi zawk te an inhrilh thin avângin, chu zû chu 'chawng zilh zu' an ti mai thin. He in chhe siam zàn hian Sadâwt thachhang daltu Tlahpawiin a pawi ziah thin.</p>
<p>Zanah i men rei chuan zingah in tho thei lo vang.</p>
<p>I fa chu thla pawh a tlin hma aṭangin i nu leh pate hnenah khan an hruai ta tlut tlut mai a. Partei pa chu i hriat angin mi taima leh hna la na chi kha a ni reng a, chutiang khawpa nau no han inhruai chu ka lo lainat thei khawp mai. Mahse, i fapa chu i chhûngte khan an duat em em thung a, an theihpatawpin an enkawl a, an pawm seilian a ni ringawt mai. Chuti phei chu ni lo se dam pawh a dam khawchhuak kher awm lo asin.</p>
<p>Tawng hi hnam khat phuar khawmtu a ni a, tawng hman a inan loh chuan inunauna nghet neih a har hle. Tawng hi a thang lian ve thei a, a hmangtute azirin a lo darhzau tawlh tawlh thin. Mizo hnahthlâk hnam hrang hrang tam tak chuan atirah hmang chiah lo mah se Lusei tawng hi an hmang tam tial tial a. A hmangtu leh a tawng kalhmang pawhin a zir a ni ang e, Lusei tawng chu a lo lár zual zêl a. Tûna Mizo tawng tia kan lo sawi mai thin pawh hi a hmangtute chênna khawvel hrin a ni e kan ti lo thei lo vang. Mahni chi bing tawngkauchheh nei Ralte ch? pêng hrang hrang te, Hmar hnam chi pêng dang tam takin Mizo tawng hi bahlah hauh lovin an hmang a. Mi tam tak phei chuan an chi bing tawng chu an hre lo lehling tawh zawk a. Chu chu Mizo hnam leh a tawnga kan inpumkhat chhoh zel dan lantirtu tha tak a ni. Kan hriat angin Mizo hnahthlâk zingah Mara, Lai, Thahdo, Paihte leh chi dang dang pawhin Mizo tawng hi an hmang uar chho tawlh tawlh a. Mi ṭhenkhatin la hmang thiam chiah lo mah se, a insuihkhawm zâwnga kan hnam pum hi kan than zêl theihna tûra Mizo tawng awmze neia kan tihhausak zêlna turah ngaih mai a remchang awm e. Mizo hnahthlâk hnam peng hrangte hi eng zat nge kan nih a, tawng tesêp chi hrang eng zat nge kan hman tih ringawt pawh hi chhui chhuah tham a ni a. Hnam khata inchhâl thei tur chinte hian tawng pakhat kan hmang dawn a nih chuan Mizo tawng hmangtute chu kan thahnem tham ve hle tawh ang a. Mizo nih manah Mizo tawng ngei hman ho hi chu thil châkawm tak a ni bawk.</p>
<p>Naupang chuan thil hi an ning thuai zel thin.</p>

<p>Thuama chuan fatu-naah chemtê a rawn pâi.</p>
<p>A nih leh, Vankhama ṭawngkam hman ṭhenkhat chun lem, laihang, hnun um dar dawi leng, kirtiang tihte hian Mizo hla thu an tihhausak phah kan ti thei ang em? Amaherawhchu hla phuahtu dangten heng a thu hmante hi an hman larpui a, hman tlang lawn atan kan neih tak tak hma chuan mi mal ṭawngkam bik a ni reng ang a, kan tawng a tihhausakna a awm hran lo vang. Sap hla phuah thiam hmingthang ṭhenkhatte pawhin hetiang hian ṭawngkam chher thar an lo ching thin a, mahse tam tak chu hman tlanglawn hlawh lovin theihnghilh leh mai a ni. <p>Kan nihlawhna em em lehna chu kan Zosapte khan Roman hawrawp zul zuia min siamsak mawlh kha a ni. <p>Damlo chu chawl lovin an khaw ṭhenawm lehlamah chuan an zawn ta zel a, chumi khua chu mi ruang inchuhah an hneh tawh thin a ni a. <p>Independent kan lo nih khan mipui aiawh Parliament member te, State Legislative Assembly member te kan lo nei ta a. Parliamentary democracy-ah chuan mipui aiawhte chu lal zawk an lo ni ta a. <p>Khai, ti rawh, i lut ve tawh em em ang, ka pute pawhin mak min ti ang e, ka han ti a.</p>
<p>Thup tum lo mai la, i zêp rei thei dawn chuang lo, i mitmeng chauh ni loin, i ke pên zangkhai dan te, i aw-ka reng aṭang pawhin. Chu bakah bual ina i zai dan rût rût kha nidang hun dang ang hek lo maw le. Hmuh deh hriat thar zawng i nei a ni phawt mai.</p>
<p>Kapi hi a beng a ngawng tawh a, ring taka biak hi a ngai thin a. Ka pu pawh hi a chak lo tawh viau a nia. Tikhan, thingpui lo in la, an mutna tur te ka han buaipui ang e aw.</p>

<p>Ngaihzawng tak tak la nei ngai lo a ni a, patling pui e ti lovin a ṭhiante bulah pawh hian a sawi chhuak ngam ngût nâ maw chu? Mahse, a tih ngai rêng rêng loh, a kut khûr kan hmuh hlau va a thuhrûk leh thin aṭang te, tûl hauh lova a ke a phûttir fo dan aṭang te, a hmui a liah chhên dan aṭangte khan kan thianpa lehkha thiama hi Petera tluk zetin hmeichhe hmaah a tlâwm ta tih ka man thiam a, eng pawh ni se tihdanglam chuan a awm hrim hrim, chu chu phat theih a ni lo. <p>A nih, tu hmeichhe thlân atan nge i tih?</p>
<p>Thil hming kan sak dâna hnam dang an lo inrawlh dân chi dang leh chu, an thil hming vuah kha kan tawnga la lûtin an thu kha kan tawng anga hmangin kan hawh ta a. A lam dân erawh chu awlsam deuh zâwk tûrin tlêmin kan tidang fo thîn a ni. Tichuan, Sâpho hnên aṭanga kan chhawm skul, pen, pensil, pin, nambar tih leh thu dang tam tak an awm a. Sâptawngte pawh hi an dictionary lian aṭanga an thu bul leh bâl han chhui chuan, an thu hman tam tak lo inṭanna hi Greek tawng te, Hebrai tawng te, Latin tawng te, French tawng te, German tawng te leh tawng dang chi hrang hrang an ni thluah mai. Old English an tih tak, an tawng bulha aṭanga thu lo piang hi a tlêm zâwk emaw tih mai tûrin a lang a ni.</p>
<p>Ka uai hniang e, chhawl thing hnuaiah e.</p>

Anni chu an neinung lutuk, hnathawk lo pawhin an awm thei, mahse hmanni kan titi dunna aṭangin mahni theih tawk hna thawh a tum tawhzia a sawi a, kan thlo dun dawn.</p>
<p>Kan hotunu chu a vaṭang nasa ve.</p>
<p>Mihring thluak hi a mak khawp mai a. Vawiin thlengin khawvel mithiamte chuan an la chhuichhuak thei lo. Thluak vei lamah chuan chutiang khatiang, dinglamah chuan chutiang khatiang chu a awm tih chu an hre ngei mai. Mahse mihring kan nihna tilangtu heng-lawmna, lungngaihna, buaina, hlimna, thinrimna, itsikna, thikna- kan neih te hi vawiina historians-te chuan an hmang ṭangkai ta daih zawk a. Tehkhinna sawi lawk ila. Rambuai lai chungchang kan lo zir ta a. Kan kawm duh pawh kum upa lam, kum 60 chunglam an ni vek tawh a. Kan zawhnaṭang diak diak khan min chhang kher lovang. Anmahni in an hriatrengnaa thil la awm, anmahni tihlimtu emaw chakna pe thintu emaw kha hriatreng a theihnghilh loh an duh a. Chutih rualin anmahni tina nawntu leh lo titap thintu thil chu hriatreng an duh lova, theihnghilh an tum tlat thin. Mihring mizia a ni. Kan duh lai kan hrereng a, kan duh lai kan theihnghilh bawk thin.</p>
